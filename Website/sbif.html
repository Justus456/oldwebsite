Eligible Expenses

Any TIF-eligible improvement which permits a building owner to attract new commercial or industrial tenants, allows an eligible business owner to maintain or expand operations, or contributes to the improved appearance and viability of a property may be funded by the Program. This includes, at DPD’s discretion, funding for the rehabilitation, remodeling or renovation of improvements including, but not limited to, the following:

  • Roof and facade
  • Alterations or structures needed for ADA compliance
  • HVAC and other mechanical systems
  • Plumbing and electrical work
  • Permanent interior renovations, including fixtures
  • Solar panels
  • Certain project-related architectural and construction management fees
  • Components of signs or awnings which are permanently affixed to the building; NOTE: This does not include any business-specific elements, including canvas or signing with the business name or logo affixed.

Ineligible Expenses

The following items are not eligible for reimbursement, and therefore will not be counted toward total project cost (this is not an exhaustive list):

  • New construction
  • Equipment-related expenses
  • Fencing
  • Parking lot construction or repair
  • Work on the interior of residential units
  • Planters surrounding or affixed to buildings
  • Landscaping
  • Work, in DPD’s sole determination, that is not consistent with the goals of this Program or the redevelopment priorities of the relevant TIF area
